Hack-Xpress

Phase I: The teams need to submit their idea synopsis while filling the registration form. In the synopsis, they need to mention their idea and describe the idea and the implementation methodology. The top 25 teams will be selected on the basis of their idea synopsis to come for the on-site hackathon on the designated day.

Phase II: Registration of the arrived teams on the day of the Hackathon. Post-registration welcome presentation by the mentors for the teams. The Hackathon will proceed according to the instructions and guidelines provided by the mentors in the presentation.

Rules

  1. Allowed team size is 2-4.
  2. At the time of registration all team members are requested to carry a valid College ID to verify their details.
  3. Changes in teams are allowed with prior permission.You may contact us for the same.
  4. Last Date of Registrations is 4th January 2020.
  5. Timings of Hackathon: 8th January - 9AM to 5PM & 9th January - 9AM onwards

Judgement Criteria

  1. Technical Complexity
  2. Extent of Implementation of Idea
  3. USP
  4. Practicality
  5. Relevance to Theme
  6. Buisness Potential

FAQ

  1. What is the time for reporting?

    Ans. The time for reporting is 8:30 AM on day 1 and 9:00 AM on day 2.

  2. Do I need to stay overnight in the college?

    Ans. No. The event ends at 5:30 PM on day 1 and resumes at 9:00 AM on day 2.

  3. What is the maximum team size?

    Ans. The maximum size of a team is 4.

  4. Will good internet connection be provided?

    Ans. Yes. We will be providing WiFi connectivity. But it is advisable to bring your 3G/4G connections.

  5. What tech stack do we have to use?

    Ans. No restrictions. You can work on any tech stack of your choice.

  6. Will lunch be provided?

    Ans. Yes, Lunch will be provided on day 1.
